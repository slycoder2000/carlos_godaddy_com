App Index

  • 12 Line Calculator - This is a simple program that adds 12 items together and stores them in the browser cache with localstorage. The checkbox to the left currently serves no purpose, but the checkbox on the right gives you the option to add or omit line items from the total, for goal or notation purposes.

  • FoodBank Listings - This is a listing of foodbanks in the Denver Metro area. Using PHP, a flag is added to determine if the location is opened or closed for the current day. This does take into account, for instance, first and third Thursday of the month.

  • Phonetic app - This program is used to convert any phrase to military words to keep from getting confused with other letters.

  • The Duel In Rhyme - The rhyme from Cyrano de Bergerac. A work in progress.
